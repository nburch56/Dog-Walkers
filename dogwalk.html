This is a guide on how to walk your dog.

Steps for walking your dog:

  1. Put leash on dog.
  2. Take dog outside.
  3. Walk around.
  4. Repeat Step 3 until dog is tired.
  5. Go back inside.
  6. Take leash off of dog.
